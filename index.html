Project Idea

Develop a Smartphone App

The App would collect information or provide update to date news on publicly listed companies selected by the user. The motivation would be to help people manage their investments.

The main feature of the App would be to provide the likelihood of a company’s stock price to rise or fall on any given day.

The App would have to be able to search through recent publications and news items and determine whether or not they were relevant to the companies being monitored by each user. It would then need to be able to calculate the probability of the information/news affecting the stock price of the company.

If this was successful and effective, people could better manage their investments and would not need to be so active in managing their portfolios.
